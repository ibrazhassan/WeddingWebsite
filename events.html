Hassan's Haldi – September 7th, 2025

In South Asian Muslim traditions, the Haldi ceremony is a joyful pre-wedding ritual where turmeric paste is applied to the groom. It's believed to purify and bless him before the big day. This lively event is filled with laughter, family bonding, and heartfelt blessings. RSVPs will be sent out closer to the event date.

Minha's Mayoun – September 10th, 2025

The Mayoun marks the bride's transition into wedding festivities. Traditionally, it's a serene gathering where the bride begins her beauty regimen and is adorned with love and blessings. A cherished tradition in South Asian Muslim households. Location TBD. RSVPs will be sent out closer to the event date.

Our Mehndi night is inspired by a mela — a festive explosion of colors, music, and dance! Expect traditional rasams, vibrant décor, and joyful celebration with loved ones. This celebration will be held at Oakville Legacy.
