Our Wedding Day

In the Name of Allah, the most Gracious, the most Merciful.

Our wedding day marks the culmination of our love story and the beautiful beginning of our shared future.

The heart of our wedding day is the Nikkah, the Islamic marriage ceremony where we come together in the presence of loved ones and in the eyes of Allah.
It's a moment of immense spiritual significance, filled with blessings and joy.

Following the ceremony, join us for the Reception, an evening of love, laughter, and new beginnings.

We can't wait to celebrate with you as we step into this new chapter of our lives!

Dress Code

Please dress to impress! For the Nikkah & Reception, we kindly request traditional South Asian formal wear or elegant evening attire. Shades of gold or cream are encouraged to complement the theme.
